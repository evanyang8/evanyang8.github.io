Types of Rats

There are 56 known species of rats. Here are some of my favorites!



Brown Rat

The brown rat is the often considered the common rat and is found on all continents except Antartica.

Manx Rat

Manx rats are charaterized by their lack of tail or short stubby tail, which results in worse balance.





Sphynx Rat

They're hairless... weird

Bristle Coat Rat

These rats can be identified by their coarse coat that has the texture of a wire brush





Powder Blue Rat

Powder Blue rats are characterized by their light blue fur
